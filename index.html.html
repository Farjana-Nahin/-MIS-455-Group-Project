Proposed Project & Objective

Student Tourism Agency Web Platform : A visual overview of the final project idea for MIS-455.

Project Objective

To develop a dynamic, student-focused e-commerce website specializing in budget-friendly travel, serving as a centralized platform for browsing, booking, and managing domestic and international travel packages exclusively scheduled during academic semester breaks.

Key Features

  • Domestic & International package categories.
  • Student-friendly pricing & fixed break-time schedules.
  • Inquiry-based booking (no online payment in this phase).
  • “Send Message” contact form for direct questions and quick replies.

Tech Stack

Frontend: HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript. The inquiry form will be integrated with EmailJS (JavaScript SDK) to route client messages from the website directly to the agency’s email address.

Expected Output

A fully functional, attractive, and student-friendly web application that acts as the primary sales and marketing channel of a student tourism agency, improving client engagement and streamlining the initial inquiry process.

How the Platform Works

Step 1
Browse Packages

Students explore Domestic and International packages filtered by destination, budget, and semester break dates.

Step 2
Send Inquiry

From the website, they fill out a simple booking inquiry form. EmailJS sends the details directly to the agency’s inbox.

Step 3
Receive Response

The agency follows up with final confirmation, payment details and any extra information outside the system (phone/email).
